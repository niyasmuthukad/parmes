About PARMES

Plastic and Air Regeneration Mission for Earth Sustainability

Our Vision

To create a cleaner, healthier, and sustainable planet by regenerating ecosystems, reducing plastic waste, and improving air quality for communities globally.

Our Mission

To innovate, recycle, and restore the Earth by implementing scalable solutions for plastic waste management and atmospheric purification, while empowering communities and promoting environmental awareness.

The Problem We Address

Global Waste Accumulation: Plastic waste is accumulating globally, polluting our land and oceans at an alarming rate.

Declining Air Quality: Air pollution is rising in urban and industrial areas, severely affecting human health and biodiversity.

The Market Gap: Few integrated solutions exist to address both plastic recycling and air quality improvement together. We are here to fill that gap with a sustainable, long-term model.

What We Do

We implement a dual-action approach to tackle pollution head-on.

Plastic Recycling

We manage the full lifecycle of plastic waste, from collection and segregation to conversion. Our process transforms harmful plastics into eco-friendly products or raw materials for industry, closing the loop on waste.

  • Community collection drives
  • Advanced material segregation
  • Upcycling into new products

Air Regeneration

We actively improve air quality in polluted areas. By installing cutting-edge air-purifying systems and planting urban green belts, we filter harmful pollutants and restore clean air to communities.

  • Urban air-purification systems
  • Reforestation and green belt planting
  • Pollution monitoring and R&D

Community Engagement

Change starts with awareness. We run extensive campaigns, school programs, and local clean-up drives to educate and empower communities, turning local action into global impact.

  • Educational workshops
  • School partnership programs
  • Volunteer clean-up events

Innovation & Research

We are committed to finding tomorrow's solutions. Our R&D team focuses on developing new sustainable materials, biodegradable products, and more efficient technologies to reduce future plastic waste and emissions.

  • Biodegradable material research
  • AI-powered pollution monitoring
  • Partnerships with research institutions
